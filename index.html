Welcome to Inha-United@Home

Advancing home robotics research with Inha University and Rainbow Robotics.

About Us

Inha-United@Home is a collaborative research initiative focusing on intelligent domestic service robotics. Our mission is to integrate vision-language understanding, manipulation, and human-centered interaction into real-world robotic systems.

Research Focus

  • Vision-Language Grounding for Object Understanding
  • Operational Space Control for Home Manipulation
  • Teleoperation and Human-in-the-Loop Learning
  • Collaborative Manipulation using RB-Y1 Platform
