Research

Our work focuses on integrating vision, language, and control in real-world service robots. We aim to build reproducible systems that can perceive, plan, and act intelligently.

  • Vision-Language Grounding for Object Understanding
  • Operational Space Control for Manipulation
  • Reinforcement Learning in Real-to-Sim Environments
